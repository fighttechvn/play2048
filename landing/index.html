How to play

Simple to start, hard to put down.

1

Slide the tiles

Swipe (or use arrow keys / WASD). All tiles slide in that direction.

2

Merge the numbers

Two tiles with the same number merge into one worth double. Each move spawns a new tile.

3

Reach 2048

Combine your way up to the 2048 tile — then keep going to beat your best score.

Ready to merge?

Play instantly in your browser, or install go2048 on your phone.
