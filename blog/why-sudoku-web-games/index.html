Sudoku in 10 seconds

Nine rows, nine columns, nine little 3×3 boxes. Fill every one with the numbers 1 to 9 — no repeats anywhere. That's it. That's the game.

And here's the twist: there's zero math involved. It's pure logic. Lock in one number and the next one basically points at itself. String enough of those tiny "ohhh, there it is" moments together and boom — solved.
